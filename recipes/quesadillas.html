Quesadillas

Quesadillas are an amazingly tasty, creamy, and savory dish to make at home. This recipe is a popular Mexican delicacy that is not purely cheese based and adds zucchini blossoms to give them a more textured flavor. Follow the steps ahead to learn how to make this Mexican favorite dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon bacon grease
  • 2 corn tortillas
  • 2 slices Manchego cheese
  • 6 zucchini blossoms

Steps

  1. Melt bacon grease in a cast iron or regular skillet over medium heat. Place tortillas side by side into the skillet. Top each tortilla with one Manchego cheese slice and three zucchini blossoms. Fold tortillas in half.
  2. Cook until tortillas are lightly browned and cheese starts to melt, 2 to 3 minutes. Turn and cook until the other side is crispy, lightly browned, and the cheese has melted, 1 more minute.
